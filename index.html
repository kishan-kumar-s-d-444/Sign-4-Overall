User Guides

Learn how to use SignSpeak's features effectively and understand their current limitations

Text to Sign Guide

How to Use:
  1. Enter text in the input field (max 70 characters)
  2. Only letters and spaces will be translated
  3. Click "Translate to Sign Language" button
  4. Switch between Image and Avatar modes
Limitations:
  • Currently only supports individual letters, not complete ASL grammar
  • Special characters and numbers may not translate correctly
  • Avatar mode requires more processing power

Image to Sign Guide

How to Use:
  1. Click "Choose Image" button to upload a photo
  2. Select an image showing a clear hand sign
  3. Click "Analyze Sign Language" button
  4. Wait for the processing to complete
Tips for Best Results:
  • Use good lighting with a plain background
  • Ensure your hand fills a good portion of the image
  • Position your hand clearly with fingers visible
  • Only one hand should be visible in the frame
Limitations:
  • Only detects ASL letters (A-Z)
  • Recognition accuracy varies based on image quality
  • Cannot interpret complex or dynamic signs

Webcam Recognition Guide

How to Use:
  1. Click "Enable Camera" button
  2. Allow browser camera permissions
  3. Position your hand in the camera view
  4. Hold each sign steady for recognition
Tips for Best Results:
  • Use good lighting conditions
  • Keep a clean background behind your hand
  • Position yourself approximately 1-2 feet from the camera
  • Move slowly when transitioning between signs
Limitations:
  • Recognition works best with ASL alphabet signs
  • Performance may vary across different devices and browsers
  • Currently optimized for one-handed signs only
  • May struggle with certain complex hand positions

Learn Alphabet Guide

How to Use:
  1. Browse through the alphabet reference images
  2. Click on a letter to select it for practice
  3. Enable your camera to practice the selected sign
  4. Receive instant feedback on your hand position
Practice Tips:
  • Start with simple signs like A, B, C before progressing
  • Practice each sign several times before moving to the next
  • Use the quick alphabet links to jump to specific letters
  • Toggle sound feedback on/off as needed
